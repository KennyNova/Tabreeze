# Tabreeze 60-90s Edit Script

This timeline is ready for Premiere, Resolve, or CapCut. It follows the approved sequence and transition recipe.

## Master Specs

- Duration target: `75s` (safe middle of 60-90s)
- Master format: `1920x1080`, `30fps`, Rec.709
- Music: light modern SaaS instrumental, no heavy drops
- Caption style: one-line lower-third, 5 to 8 words

## Transition Plan

- Between near-identical UI states: `200-400ms` cross-dissolve.
- Between feature groups: subtle directional slide (`300ms` max).
- Hero and closing frames: slow zoom-in (`5-8%`) over duration.
- Avoid flashy transitions that break SaaS tone.

## Audio + Motion Notes

- Keep SFX minimal: light whoosh for section changes only.
- Duck music by `-6dB` under VO.
- Use short pause before final CTA for emphasis.

## On-Screen Text Safety

- Keep captions in lower-third safe area.
- Max ~42 characters per caption line.
- Use consistent wording from `VIDEO_MESSAGING_LOCK.md`.

## Alternate 60s Cut (If Needed)

- Remove homelab section (0:53-1:00).
- Compress calendar/weather section by 2s.
- Keep full layout-edit sequence; this is the core differentiator.
